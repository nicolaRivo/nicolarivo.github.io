// About

Nicola Rivosecchi

I'm a sound designer, composer, sound mixer, audio editor, and sound artist based in London. With experience across the entire audio production workflow, I handle all areas of film sound — from recording and composing to dialogue editing, foley, and final mixing.

My approach merges artistic intuition with technical precision. I'm constantly exploring the boundaries between organic and synthetic sound, creating sonic landscapes that evoke emotion and enhance storytelling.

Whether it's atmospheric film soundscapes, interactive game audio, experimental sound installations, or generative audio systems — I bring a unique perspective shaped by my background in music, sound design, and creative coding.
