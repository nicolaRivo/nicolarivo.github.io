FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common questions about my teaching services

Do I need to have my own instrument?

Yes, having your own instrument is essential for regular practice between lessons. For beginners, I can provide guidance on instrument selection, whether you're purchasing or renting. If you're taking lessons at my studio, you'll use my instruments during the lesson.

How often should I take lessons?

For most students, weekly lessons provide the right balance of instruction and independent practice time. This consistent schedule helps maintain momentum and ensures steady progress. Some advanced students benefit from twice-weekly sessions, while occasional bi-weekly lessons can work for highly self-motivated adult learners.

How much should I practice between lessons?

Practice requirements vary by age and level, but consistency is more important than marathon sessions. For young beginners, 15-20 minutes of daily practice is appropriate. Intermediate students should aim for 30-45 minutes daily, while advanced students typically need 1-2 hours of focused practice. I'll provide specific practice strategies tailored to your level and goals.

Do you offer online lessons?

Yes, I offer high-quality online lessons using video conferencing platforms with optimized audio settings. Online lessons provide flexibility and have proven very effective for many of my students. I provide specialized resources and techniques specifically designed for the online learning environment.

What ages do you teach?

I teach students of all ages, from young children (typically starting around age 5-6) through adults of any age. My teaching approach is adapted to suit the learning style, developmental stage, and goals of each student, regardless of their age or previous experience.
